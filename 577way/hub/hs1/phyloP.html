VGP 577-way PhyloP conservation — hs1 (GCA_009914755.4)

Per-base PhyloP conservation scores from the vgp-577way-v1 Progressive Cactus alignment of 577 vertebrate genomes, anchored at hs1 (GCA_009914755.4).

Methods

Computed with cactus-phast from Cactus v3.2.0. The reference's CDS annotation was used to extract 4-fold-degenerate sites for neutral-model training; the trained model is the REV substitution model fit by phyloFit --EM --precision HIGH with modFreqs-symmetrized background frequencies; per-base scores are -log10(p) from phyloP --method LRT --mode CONACC. A --root <CladeAnc> variant restricts the entire pipeline (model training and scoring) to the named clade; a --subtree <CladeAnc> variant trains on the full tree but performs the per-base LRT only on the named clade.
